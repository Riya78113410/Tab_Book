The Universe & Solar System

About the Universe

The universe is all of space and time and their contents, including planets, stars, galaxies, and all other forms of matter and energy. The observable universe is about 93 billion light-years in diameter and is still expanding.

It began with the Big Bang approximately 13.8 billion years ago. Dark matter and dark energy make up most of the universe, and scientists are still uncovering its mysteries.

About the Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and all celestial bodies that are gravitationally bound to it. This includes eight planets, their moons, dwarf planets like Pluto, comets, asteroids, and more.
